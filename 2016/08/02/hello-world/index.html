Hi Friends! Welcome to the SubmitHealth blog.

Why we are here?

We’re here to help in the search of healthcare that is:

  1. Cost effective
  2. Time effective
  3. Get’s you patched up Effectively!

In short, we seek Effective Healthcare

My experience with the healthcare system here in America can be summarized by:

  • Lots of money lost
  • Often no real resolve to my problems
  • Only see a doctor when my symptoms include verge of death

We get it though. Understandably there isn’t a magical solution to solve everyone’s discomfort at the moment, that is until we finally get that tricorder.
But until then, our hope is to find and share practical solutions for getting healthcare to all more effectively.

So stay tuned and thank you for reading.
